An introduction about our project

We are 4 3rd year students who had to make a project about a global subject. we chose to make a website and a song about human rights in China and the Netherlands.

This website was completely build from scratch, and programmed by hand, so it cost a tremendous amount of time to assemble and looks more plain than websites build with website bulding packages.

This website was made to show the differences between the human rights in China and the Netherlands. There are multiple articles and a cover of empire state of mind on here.

We also put a charity on this website, of which you can find more information at the tab charity.
